Platform Features

More than a player.
A living ecosystem.

Every DPA™ device ships with software features that keep artists earning and fans engaged — long after the first play.

❤️

Favorites / Hearts

Fans heart tracks on the device and from the portal. Synced to firmware. Artists see which tracks fans love most — real engagement data, not algorithm guesses.

🌍

Fleet Tracker

See every device you've sold on a live map. Track activations by region, monitor play counts, and spot where your fanbase is growing — in real time.

📊

Real Analytics

Per-track play counts, listening duration, skip rates, favorite ratios. Know how fans actually experience your album — not just that they pressed play once.

🎨

LED Theme Designer

29 patterns including audio-reactive VU meters. Set your brand colors for idle, playback, and charging. Fans can customize too — their device, their vibe.

🎁

Capsule Perks (DCNP)

Push content to devices you sold years ago. Concert pre-sales, exclusive video, merch drops, remix stems, virtual signings. 6 event types. Direct to fan.

🖼️

Cover Art & Booklet

Upload album art, per-track art, credits, lyrics, and gallery images. Displayed on the device dashboard and fan portal. The full album experience, not just audio.

🔄

Secondary Marketplace

Fans trade DPA™ devices. Every resale is tracked by serial ID. Artists earn 10% royalty automatically. Limited editions appreciate. Your catalog keeps paying.

🔒

Encrypted Content

AES-256-GCM encryption locked to the device's unique serial ID. Your masters are secure at rest on the SD card. No ripping, no piracy, no compromise on quality.
