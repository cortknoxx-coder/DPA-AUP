The Problem

Streaming broke the music economy.

In the CD era, artists earned $1–2 per unit. Today, an artist needs 350,000 streams to match what 1,000 CD sales made. The money didn't disappear — it just stopped reaching creators.

💿

Streaming Renting

Fans don't own anything. Artists don't earn anything. Algorithms decide who gets heard. One platform change can erase your audience overnight.

📀

Vinyl is stuck in 1960

$1.2B market proves fans pay premium for physical music. But zero interactivity — once sold, the artist-fan connection is over. No updates, no perks, no relationship.

✦

DPA™ changes everything

A physical device that plays hi-fi audio, receives perks for life, and earns artists real money on every sale and resale. The format that should have come after CDs.

The Community

Built for creators and fans.

For Artists & Creators

  • Earn $20–50+ per unit — not $0.003 per stream
  • Upload your masters in studio quality (FLAC 24/96)
  • Push perks directly to every device you've sold — concert tickets, merch drops, exclusive remixes, video content
  • Earn 10% royalty on every secondary market resale
  • Keep 100% of your master rights. No label required
  • Your LED theme, your branding, your artist identity on the device
  • Real analytics — know how fans actually listen

For Fans & Collectors

  • Own something real — not a playlist that disappears
  • Studio-quality audio through a dedicated hi-fi DAC
  • Receive perks from your favorite artist for life — no app needed
  • 29 LED visualizer patterns react to the music in real-time
  • Bluetooth wireless to your speakers and headphones
  • Resell on the secondary market — value appreciates for limited runs
  • Connect via WiFi, NFC tap, or Bluetooth — works without internet
How It Works

From studio to fan's hands.

01

Create

Upload your album, design your LED theme, set your price. The creator portal handles everything.

02

Manufacture

We flash your music onto DPA™ devices. Order 50 or 50,000. Each unit has a unique serial ID.

03

Sell

At shows, online, in stores. You set the price. You keep the margin. No middleman.

04

Push Perks

Months or years later — push concert tickets, exclusive content, merch drops directly to every device you've sold.
