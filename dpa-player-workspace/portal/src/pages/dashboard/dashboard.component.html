Masters Storage Policy

🛡️
Your unencrypted WAV/FLAC masters are stored temporarily in a secure intake bucket. Once the DPAC (Encrypted Capsule) is successfully built, the raw source files are permanently purged from our cloud. Only the encrypted assets remain to ensure maximum security for your IP.
